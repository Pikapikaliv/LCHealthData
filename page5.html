Photos of Lawrence County, KY Observations from a Car Windshield

 

Lawrence County Judicial Center

This is a facility where many people attend court because wrongdoings have been committed.  Rulings provide order to the community, which keeps the community as safe as possible.

Louisa War Monument

This monument displays the names of people from Lawrence County who participated in the wars involving the United States.  This is a reminder to the locals that many people in the county value freedom of the American people.

Lawrence County Flea Market

This flea market opens up the opportunity for locals to sell handmade or homegrown goods or even hold a rummage sale in a central location.  This brings a crowd of people together, which creates good profit and even better fellowship. 

Vegetables, Plants, and Flowers Sale

This private business provides homegrown goods to the city of Louisa.  It is located on a busy street in downtown Louisa so it is easy to locate and obtain fresh vegetables, plants, and flowers.

 

 

Catalpa Church

This is one of several churches in Lawrence County.  Like many others, it holds several public events that are enjoyed by the community.

Truck Sales

Since the community supports coal, one can find several coal docks and facilities that sell coal trucks.  Many coal trucks race down US 23, which runs directly through Louisa.

Masterpiece Cafe & Painted Cow Art Gallery

This serves the community as a nice display of local talent.  In addition, art classes are held for people of every age.  The coffee shop provides one with a refreshment while individuals enjoy the beauty of art.

Tractor Supply Co.

Not only does this location serve as a farm supply store, but this facility also provides an event in the summer months called Trading Days, which allows locals to buy, sell, and trade animals in the parking lot.
