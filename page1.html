Strong Areas

* There are plenty of community functions and events that provide individuals with recreational activities throughout the week and sometimes on the weekend.
* County pride is seen outside of the old courthouse and within Sullivan University, which houses a beautiful museum of Louisa's war history.
* Churches act as safe places for individuals to go and share a bite to eat or send their kids for events such as lock-ins or after prom parties.
* Individuals are encouraged to share kindness with the Help Boxes in town that provide free food or books to those in need.  In addition, the Masterpiece Cafe has a Share a Cup Wall where individuals can pre-purchase a cup of coffee for one special person.

Weak Areas

* The American Electric Power facility provides a majority of the pollution in the county, which explains why it is located far from town.
* The tire yard is a hazard to the environment since these tires are burned in a location out of town.
* There are no parks in the county that are considered safe by the general public because drug deals happen frequently in them.
* The large number of fast food restaurants encourage obesity and diabetes.
